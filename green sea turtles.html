Wikipedia: Green Sea Turtles

Fun Facts about Green Sea Turtles

  • They are the largest of all sea turtle species.
  • Green sea turtles are herbivores and primarily feed on seagrass.
  • They can live for several decades.

How do Green Sea Turtles get food?

  1. They first swim to their preferred feeding grounds which can be up to 10 kilometers away.
  2. They then use their sharp beaks to graze on seagrass.
  3. The grazing of seagrass promotes the health of the marine ecosystem.
