Gray Wolves

Wikipedia: Gray Wolves

Fun Facts about Gray Wolves

  • Gray wolves are highly social animals that live in packs.
  • They are excellent hunters and can run at speeds of up to 40 miles per hour.
  • Gray wolves have a keen sense of smell and can detect prey from several miles away.

How do Gray Wolves get food?

  1. They first locate their prey using their keen sense of smell.
  2. They then work together as a pack to chase and corner the prey.
  3. Finally, they take turns attacking the prey to bring it down.
