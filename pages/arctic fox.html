Fun Facts about Arctic Foxes

  • Arctic foxes have thick, white fur that helps them blend into their snowy environment.
  • They are excellent diggers and can create complex burrow systems.
  • Arctic foxes are opportunistic feeders and will eat whatever prey is available.

How do Arctic Foxes get food?

  1. They first listen to hear if any prey is nearby.
  2. They then use their keen sense of smell to locate the prey.
  3. Finally, they pounce on the prey to catch it.
